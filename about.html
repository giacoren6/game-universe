More Than Just a Gaming Website

Game Universe was created as a place where gamers can discover new releases, revisit timeless classics and find useful information about the games they love.

We believe that every game offers a different world to explore. Our goal is to bring those worlds together in one accessible platform for PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo and PC players.
